What is the Savanna?

The savanna is a unique biome characterized by vast grasslands dotted with scattered trees. It's an ecosystem that supports a rich variety of wildlife, from majestic elephants to graceful antelopes. However, the savanna faces numerous challenges, including deforestation, climate change, and human encroachment.

Our Mission

At Heli's Savanna, our mission is to protect and restore the savanna ecosystems. We focus on preserving biodiversity, promoting sustainable practices, and raising awareness about the importance of these habitats for the global environment.

How You Can Help
